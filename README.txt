%% Agend-based model for political party competition 

Re-implementation of the model originally publiced in (version = 1):

Michael Laver: Policy and the Dynamics of Political Party Competition
American Political Science Review , Volume 99 , Issue 2 , May 2005 , pp. 263 - 281
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1017/S0003055405051646

Three own extensions were added to the model:

1) Non-voting (adujstable voting threshold for maximal distance of voters to next party; version = 2)
2) Decision rule Optimizer
3) Lobbyist and Party Member
